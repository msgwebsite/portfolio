About Me

Hi, I'm Maitreya! I'm a Data Science enthusiast with an insatiable curiosity for uncovering stories hidden within data. My passion lies in leveraging Machine Learning, Analytical Research, and Mathematical Statistics to solve complex, real-world puzzles.

I believe data is a powerful tool for innovation, and I thrive on transforming raw numbers into meaningful insights that can drive decisions and spark change. What makes me good at it? A blend of relentless problem-solving, a creative approach to algorithms, strong mathematical and statistical foundations, and a genuine love for the continuous learning journey that data science offers.

I'm currently pursuing a BS-MS degree in Data Science at IISER Thiruvananthapuram, diving deep into the fascinating worlds of machine learning, data analysis, and cutting-edge research. My academic and personal journey has been a vibrant tapestry woven with threads of curiosity, creative problem-solving, and an unwavering commitment to continuous learning.

Beyond the realm of data and algorithms, I am an avid Tabla performer, finding rhythm and expression in classical Indian music. I also actively contribute to various student communities on campus, including the Placement Cell, CMIT, and the Math Club. For me, collaboration, fostering a rich cultural environment, and nurturing creativity are just as vital as analytical rigor.
